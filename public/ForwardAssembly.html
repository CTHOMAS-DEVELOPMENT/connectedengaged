Forward Assembly

Linking people, places and solutions.

A developing network of people, venues and skills coming together to support practical, real-world remedies.

These pages are an early draft. They are not polished and they are not meant to present Forward Assembly as anything special or separate from other efforts. This is simply a shared working base that anyone can contribute to.

The aim is to make it easier for local and regional groups to coordinate, share resources, and turn ideas into action.

Draft Mission Statement (for discussion)

Forward Assembly exists to connect people, venues and practical skills across the UK and Ireland so communities can design and deliver their own remedies, independent of party politics and corporate control.

Most sections of this site are clearly marked “under construction”. They are intentionally simple so different teams can add content without barriers.
