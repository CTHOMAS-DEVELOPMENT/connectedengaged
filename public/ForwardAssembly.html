Forward Assembly

A developing national network of coordinators, venues and think tank teams working together on real-world solutions.

These pages are an early draft of the project. Much of the site is still “Under Construction”, but we want people at the November 2025 gathering to see that the foundations are being laid and that this is not just a talking shop.

Over the coming weeks we’ll be adding:

  • Regional maps and local contact structures
  • A sharable venue spreadsheet for the UK & Ireland
  • Think Tank departments and team pages
  • Spaces for projects, initiatives and events

Please explore the sections in the menu above. Many pages will simply state “Website under construction” with space for the tech / content teams to add copy.
