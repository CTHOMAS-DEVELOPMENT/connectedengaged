Introduction

There has never been a more important time for people to take responsibility for knowledge, courage and action – and to bring real power back to our communities.

Forward Assembly exists as a practical, grounded response to the direction many of us feel the world is heading. Rather than simply protesting, this initiative is about building structures, connections and remedies that work in the real world.

  • Now is the time for a concerted call to action.
  • We recognise the need for solutions, not just analysis.
  • We want greater cohesion and unity across the Freedom Movement and beyond.
  • We already have infrastructure, networks and knowledge that must not be wasted.
  • We aim to use technology in ways that serve humanity, not corporate or technocratic control.

This website is the first step in creating a national framework of coordinators, venues and think tank teams that can turn ideas into reality.
